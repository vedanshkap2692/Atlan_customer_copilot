## Strong typing¶
(source: https://developer.atlan.com/models/)

Our SDKs are strongly-typed to represent everytype's attributes and relationships directly. This reduces the burden on you to understand how these are named, capitalized, spelled, or even the type of data they possess — since all of those details are encoded directly into the models the SDKs use.

Here you will find a complete reference of all of these attributes and relationships.
